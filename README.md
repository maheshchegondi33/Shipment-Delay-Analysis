E-Commerce Shipment Delay Analysis | Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel:
Analyzed shipment data containing order, delivery, logistics, and customer information to identify factors contributing to delivery delays.
Performed data cleaning and preprocessing using Python (Pandas, NumPy) and SQL to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover trends in shipping performance across regions, product categories, and shipping modes.
Identified that delayed deliveries were concentrated in specific regions and shipping methods, highlighting operational bottlenecks.
Analyzed the relationship between order volume, shipping mode, warehouse location, and delivery time to determine key delay drivers.
Built interactive Power BI dashboards to monitor on-time delivery rate, average shipping duration, delay percentage, and regional performance.
Developed KPIs and visual reports enabling stakeholders to track logistics efficiency and customer fulfillment metrics.
Generated actionable recommendations that could help reduce shipment delays and improve customer satisfaction.

Key Insights:
Standard shipping mode experienced higher delay rates compared to expedited shipping.
Certain regions showed consistently higher delivery delays due to logistics and transportation constraints.
Peak sales periods resulted in increased shipment delays because of higher order volumes.
Orders traveling longer distances had a greater probability of delayed delivery.
Specific product categories exhibited higher delay rates due to inventory and fulfillment challenges.
A small number of distribution centers contributed disproportionately to delayed shipments.
On-time delivery performance varied significantly across shipping carriers.
